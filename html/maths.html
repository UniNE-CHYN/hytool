Hydrogeological Toolbox

Mathematical functions

A set of functions used internally to compute the solutions and the logarithmic derivatives.

ldiff Approximate logarithmic derivative with centered differences
ldiffs Approximate logarithmic derivative with Spline sampling
ldiffh Approximate logarithmic derivative with Horne formula
stefhest Stefhest inverse Laplace numerical transform
dehoog de Hoog inverse Laplace numerical transform
lin_fit Linear least squares
nli_fit Non linear least squares
